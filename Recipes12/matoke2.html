steps

  • Peel the bananas and wash them in cold water.
  • Put into a pot, add the water, and cover.
  • Boil for 10 minutes, then reduce the heat and continue to cook. ...
  • Drain the water
  • Wrap the mashed food in foil and place on a steaming basket.
  • Place the steamer inside a large pot and add water.
  • Serve while hot
